UiKit V2 Icon component documentation Tier 1

  • Tier 1 Icons are most commonly used at 24x24. They can be used at 16x16 or 20x20, 32x32 etc. Dimensions must be scaled up in increments of 8px after reaching 24x24.
  • By default, Tier 1 Icons are colored neutral/slate 400.
  • Tier 1 Icons have the ability to have other colors applied to it such as semantic/red, semantic/green, primary/blue 400 etc.
  • Tier 1 Icons can be colored white but must be on a colored background with sufficient contrast for ADA compliance.

Tier 2

  • Tier 2 Icons are most commonly used at 32x23. They can be used at 24x24 or 32x32 etc. Dimensions must be scaled up increments of 8px after reaching 24x24.
  • Tier 2 Icons are colored neutral/slate 400 or duo colored with a primary/blue 200 accent.
  • Tier 2 Icons have the ability to change color to primary/blue 400 in a selected state.

Mono

  • Mono The default color is neutral/slate 400.

Colored with Accent

  • Colored with Accent Colored neutral/slate 400 or duo colored with a primary/blue 200 accent.
  • If used in a selected state, the entire icon changes color to primary/blue 400.

Tier 3

  • Tier 3 Icons are most commonly used at 40x40. They can be used as illustrations in empty states.
  • When used in a selected state, the icon does not change color.
